## App Description

SnapPDF turns your iPhone into a powerful document scanner. Just point your camera at any page, snap a photo, and get a clean, professional PDF in seconds.

**Smart Scanning**
Automatic edge detection finds the document boundary instantly. Fine-tune the crop by dragging the corner handles, and SnapPDF applies perspective correction to straighten the page — even if you shot it at an angle.

**Image Enhancement**
Choose from four filter modes to make your scans look their best:
• Auto — brightens, sharpens, and evens out lighting automatically
• Original — keeps the raw photo as-is
• Grayscale — clean gray tones for mixed content
• B&W — high-contrast black and white, ideal for text documents

Need more control? Use the brightness, contrast, and sharpness sliders to dial in the perfect look.

**Multi-Page PDFs**
Scan multiple pages and combine them into a single PDF. Rotate, delete, or reorder pages before saving.

**OCR Text Extraction**
Extract text from any scanned document using on-device text recognition. Copy, share, or search the extracted text — no internet connection required.

**Easy Sharing**
Share your PDFs via AirDrop, email, Messages, or any other app. Save directly to Files, or keep your scans organized in SnapPDF's built-in document library.

**Privacy First**
All processing happens on your device. No data is collected, uploaded, or shared. Period.
